About Pasifika.xyz

Pasifika.xyz is a pioneering Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network (DePIN) specifically designed for Pacific Island communities. Our mission is to create a sustainable economic ecosystem that preserves cultural heritage while creating new digital opportunities for islanders.

PASIFIKA Token (PSF)

Our native token powers the entire Pasifika ecosystem, providing incentives for infrastructure providers, content creators, and community participants.

Built on Linea's zkEVM Layer-2 solution for our proof-of-concept, while evaluating alternatives like Arbitrum, zkSync Era, StarkNet, Base, Polygon zkEVM, RootStock, and Internet Computer for production deployment.

Key Components of Our DePIN

Physical Infrastructure

Solar-powered mesh networks, satellite connectivity nodes, and local data centers distributed across Pacific Islands.

Token Economics

Incentive structures to reward infrastructure providers, validators, content creators, and community participants.

Governance

Community-led decision making with special emphasis on preserving cultural heritage and local autonomy.

Sustainability

Renewable energy focus with solar panels, sustainable materials, and minimal environmental impact.

Community Hubs

Digital access centers that serve as education spaces, work hubs, and community gathering places.

Global Integration

Connecting isolated communities to global markets, education, and healthcare resources.

Network Architecture
