About Me

Humanitarian Mentor Advisor Innovator Activist Software Engineer Prompt Engineer AI ML Web3 Blockchain Smart Contracts GIS Geomatics Bounty Writer

I am the Founder and Full Stack Developer of Pasifika.xyz, a decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) designed specifically for Pacific Island communities. Our platform uses the PASIFIKA token (PSF) with a proof of concept built on Linea's zkEVM Layer-2 solution, while evaluating alternatives like Arbitrum, zkSync Era, StarkNet, Base, Polygon zkEVM, RootStock, and Internet Computer for production.

My mission is to create a sustainable economic ecosystem that preserves cultural heritage while creating new digital opportunities for islanders throughout the Pacific region.
